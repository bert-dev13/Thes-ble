      Applies to Summary of Respondents, Findings, and Conclusions. Recommendations do not include implication sentences.

      Summary of Respondents

      Generates one paragraph summarizing all saved profile tables (Age, Gender, Civil Status, Education, Position, Rating, Years in Service, In-Service). Combines dominant categories in the required sequence—no table numbers, frequencies, or multiple paragraphs.

      Summary of Findings

      Automatically generates findings from saved profile tables, Likert tables, executive summaries, and statistical tests. Profile: dominant categories per respondent group. Likert: indicators under highest qualitative description (e.g., Very High, Fully Manifested). Executive summaries: domains by QD in descending order. T-tests: formal finding comparing computed vs critical value. Uses varied academic openings and quotation marks for indicators.

      Conclusion

      Generates conclusions strictly based on the generated findings. No new ideas or recommendations.
